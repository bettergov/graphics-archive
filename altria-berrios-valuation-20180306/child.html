Altria’s shifting assessment

In September 2017, Cook County Assessor Joseph Berrios’ office slashed the real estate assessment on a 500,000-square-foot building owned by the U.S. Smokeless Tobacco Company, part of the Altria Group represented by Berrios’ lobbying firm. The value of the break was calculated based on a valuation for the property that was half of what it soon sold for.

Initial valuation: $12.3 million

After appeal: $8.1 million

Value property sold for: $16 million

Est. property tax from initial valuation

$1.1 million

Est. property tax after appeal

$725,000

Est. property tax from value sold for

$1.4 million

Source: Cook County Assessor’s Office
