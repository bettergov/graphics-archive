Separate measures, same problem

Two official measures of the state’s end-of-year fiscal health show Illinois’ general funds account has long operated in the red, but declined dramatically in the last two years during a historic budget impasse.

Source: FY 2018 Economic Forecast and Revenue Estimate and FY 2017 Revenue Update, p. 33 (Budgetary basis); Illinois Comptroller Comprehensive Annual Financial Report Fiscal Year 2017, p. 25 (GAAP basis); Illinois Comptroller Comprehensive Annual Financial Report Fiscal Year 2009, p. 12 (GAAP basis)
