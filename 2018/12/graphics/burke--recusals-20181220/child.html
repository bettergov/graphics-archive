The King of Recusals

Chicago aldermen who think they have a conflict of interest or an appearance of a conflict often invoke the parliamentary procedure known as a “Rule 14” when voting on such matters.

No alderman does that more than Ald. Ed Burke, 14th, whose Rule 14 votes account for 81 percent of all such recusals since 2011.

Source: City Council records, Jan. 2011 through Nov. 2018
