If Illinois taxed services like other states

By its broadest estimate, the Illinois Commission on Government Forecasting & Accountability estimates that Illinois could boost its annual sales tax revenue by nearly $3 billion if it taxed as many services as Iowa.

Notes: Models assume a 5% tax rate, a 7/1/17 implementation date, and a 67%, 75%, and 90% compliance timeline.

Source: Service Taxes 2017 Update, p.11, Commission on Government Forecasting & Accountability, Illinois General Assembly
