What it does
All schools K-12 School Safety Drill Amendment Schools must conduct a law enforcement-led active shooter drill every year.
Rising ninth grade students School Code Dental Examination Amendment Requires all children entering the ninth grade of any school to have a dental examination. Currently, this is only required of students entering kindergarten, second grade and sixth grades.
Nursing mothers Jury Duty Exemption Amendment Nursing moms may request to be excused from jury duty.
