What it does
Driver’s license applicants Vehicle Code “Dutch Reach” Amendment A new safety measure will be added to the driver’s license written exam that advises drivers to use their right hand to open their door, making them more aware of bicycles and other oncoming traffic.
Individuals with suspended licenses Vehicle Code Suspended License Amendment Lowers the penalty for driving with a suspended driver’s license to a petty offense if the suspension was for unpaid parking fines, automated camera enforcement or unpaid child support.
Children under two years of age Child Passenger Protection Amendment Toddlers under two years old must ride in rear-facing car seats.
