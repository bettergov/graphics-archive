Chicago’s declining recycling rate

Since Mayor Rahm Emanuel’s Blue Cart recycling program went citywide in 2013, the percent of residential waste recycled has dropped from more than 11 percent in 2014 to less than 9 percent in the first six months of 2018.

Note: 2018 data through June; only residential buildings with fewer than five units are included in the rate

Source: Dept. of Streets and Sanitation
