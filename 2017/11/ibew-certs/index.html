An internal investigation by City Colleges of Chicago’s inspector general suggests that all the diplomas offered by Richard J. Daley College to aspiring electricians are invalid because the programs were never properly approved, included ineligible students and suffered from a total lack of oversight.
Notes: Graduation rate is calculated using full time students who begin their studies in the fall and complete within three years.
