Metra fare calculator

Select a train line, departure and arrival station to see old, current and proposed rates for the trip. First enter the train line. Then search stations by name or by zone.

Notes: Metra's proposed fare increases include: a 25 cent increase on all one-way tickets; 10-ride tickets priced 9.5 times the one-way ticket price (up from 9 times); monthly passes priced 29 times the one-way ticket price (up from 28.5 times). Metra also proposes weekend passes increase from $8 to $10 (not shown).
